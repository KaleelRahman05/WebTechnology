AI and AI Tools

About AI:

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems. AI tools are specialized software applications that leverage these capabilities to perform tasks such as data analysis, natural language processing, image recognition, and decision-making. From virtual assistants and chatbots to advanced systems for medical diagnostics and autonomous vehicles, AI tools are transforming industries by enhancing efficiency, accuracy, and scalability. These tools often use machine learning algorithms to improve over time, adapting to new data and evolving user needs.
